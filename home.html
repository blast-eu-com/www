Blast is an Open Source agentless software scheduling and executing jobs scenarios for all types of cloud solutions. It allows you to manage and administer your scheduled jobs via a friendly frontend connected to a backend itself connected to Elasticsearch. Blast UI offers to the operators the capability to execute recurrent/one-shot jobs on one/serveral nodes sequencially/simultaneously. additionally you can browse through your scripts, playbooks and execution results.

Blast automation platform improves engineers collaborating.

Blast comes with the possibility to share your community work with others. This capability increases the collaboration between communities and speed up the delivery by reducing the workload.
